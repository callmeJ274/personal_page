Level Description

My mother language is Vietnamese so I'm definitely a native speaker. In fact, I pround of it since I discovered myself as a language nerd. Like other languages, we have lots of accents. Honestly, I like guys with the Ha Noi accent (from the Northern Vietnam) the most as it's somehow reliable to me, especially with the low tone. The Center's accents are quite confused to me and I guess many people in my country would feel the same. I have some friends at my university comming from the Center, sometimes I could not understand what they are talking but anyway, I love their accents too. It sounds very romantic, Hue or Quang Ngai for example. Going to the Southern Vietnam where I'm living now, many people including myself fall in love with the Southwest accent girls as they speak very gently and softly even when they speak some swear words or yell at somebody.
